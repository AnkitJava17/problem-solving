Scenario: You work for a grocery store, and you have been tasked with 
creating an algorithm to calculate the total cost of a customer’s purchase,
including taxes and discounts. The store offers a discount of 10% on purchases
over $50 and charges a sales tax rate of 8%.​

1. Start
2. Take input purchase
3. Declare discount to 0.1 and tax to 0.08
4. If purchase greater than 50 then go to step 5 else go to step 7
5. Declare discounted to purchase multiplied by discount
6. Subtract discounted from purchase
7. Declare taxedPrice to purchase multiplied by tax
8. Add taxedPrice to purchase
9. Print purchase
10. End
